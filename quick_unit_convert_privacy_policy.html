Privacy Policy for Quick Unit Convert

Quick Unit Convert

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

NexaBase LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Quick Unit Convert mobile application ("App").

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use Quick Unit Convert.

1. Information We Collect

Quick Unit Convert is a simple unit conversion app. The App does not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide personal information.

The App itself does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, precise location, or user-generated files.

The App may store basic app preferences locally on your device, such as appearance settings or recently used conversion options. These preferences remain on your device and are not sent to our servers.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Quick Unit Convert uses Google AdMob to display ads.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app interactions, crash diagnostics, and other data as described in Google's own policies. This information may be used to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can manage ad personalization through your device settings or Google account settings.

3. How We Use Information

We use locally stored app preferences only to provide basic app functionality, such as remembering display settings or recently used conversion options.

Advertising data handled by Google AdMob is used by Google and its partners to provide and improve advertising services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We do not share personal information with third parties, except that the App uses Google AdMob, which may collect and process data according to Google's policies.

5. Data Retention

Quick Unit Convert does not maintain user accounts or store personal information on our servers.

Local app preferences remain on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the App.

Data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's own data retention policies.

6. Data Security

Because Quick Unit Convert does not collect account information or store personal data on our servers, we do not maintain a user database for this App.

We rely on platform security controls provided by Android and third-party services such as Google AdMob for data handled by those services.

7. Children's Privacy

Quick Unit Convert is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can review the issue.

8. User Choices

You may uninstall the App at any time.

You may clear locally stored app data through your Android device settings.

You may manage advertising preferences through your device settings or Google account settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
